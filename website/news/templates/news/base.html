What we do.

We are living in unprecedented times and every person in this world has been impacted by natural disasters in some way or the other. We may not be able to change the past but we can act constructively towards the betterment of the current situation, 'Precaution is better than cure'. To be above this uncertain dilemma of life and death situations, we have built this system to help manage disaster situations, spread awareness and save lives.
